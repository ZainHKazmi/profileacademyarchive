How do I create a Facebook event?

To create a private or public event:

  1. Click Events in the left menu of your News Feed
  2. Click Create in the top right
  3. Click Create Private Event to choose between a private or public event
  4. Fill in the event name, details, location and time
  5. Click Create

You'll be taken to your event where you can invite guests, upload photos, share posts and edit event details.

Note: You're not able to change the Privacy Settings once you've created the event.
